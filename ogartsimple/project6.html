BookCover Series

Tool(s): Adobe Photoshop, Illustration

Project Description: The BookCover Series project began with thorough research into the content and essence of the book, ensuring a deep understanding of its narrative in mind and immersing it into the design. Adobe Photoshop becomes the key tool in translating ideas into visuals, where the general concept of the book cover takes shape. This phase involves a calculated consideration of color palettes and hierarchical structures, aligning with the thematic essence of each book in the series.
